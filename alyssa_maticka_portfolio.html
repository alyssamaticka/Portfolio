Robotics · Space

HarveStar · Lunar Robotic Harvester

Analysis and Report Coordinator · Canadian Space Agency (OASYS)

Developed a robotic end-effector for autonomous crop harvesting in a lunar greenhouse under Canadian Space Agency mentorship. The system uses a three-gear actuation and four-bar linkage design with rubber gripper pads, capable of harvesting radishes, carrots, and tomatoes. Wrote Python (CircuitPython) firmware for a 4-DOF robotic arm with inverse kinematics, smooth multi-joint motion interpolation, and an 18-zone joint constraint safety system.

Python / CircuitPython Inverse Kinematics 4-DOF Arm Stress Analysis Mechanical Design
